**Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism meets Tech Minimalism

**Core Principles**:
- Bold, unapologetic geometric shapes with sharp edges and strong borders
- High-contrast color blocking with vibrant accent colors against neutral bases
- Asymmetric layouts that break traditional grid constraints
- Raw, honest presentation of UI elements without excessive decoration

**Color Philosophy**: 
Start with a stark monochrome foundation (deep charcoal #1a1a1a and crisp white #ffffff), then inject electric blue (#0066ff) and neon cyan (#00d9ff) as strategic accent colors. The palette communicates technological precision while maintaining approachability. Colors are used in large, confident blocks rather than gradients.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Embrace diagonal cuts and overlapping sections. Hero content sits at a dramatic angle with clipped edges. Service cards break out of traditional grids with intentional misalignment. Content sections use z-index layering to create depth through overlap rather than shadows.

**Signature Elements**:
- Thick 3-4px borders on interactive elements with sharp corners
- Diagonal clip-path cuts on section transitions (polygon cuts at 8-12 degree angles)
- Oversized typography with mixed weights (900 for headings, 400 for body)

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Interactions are direct and immediate. Hover states shift colors boldly (not subtle fades). Buttons have strong press states with transform: scale(0.98). Scrolling reveals content with slide-in animations from unexpected angles.

**Animation**: 
Fast, snappy transitions (150-200ms). Elements enter with transform combinations (translateX + rotate). No easing curves—use linear or steps() for mechanical precision. Stagger animations for card grids with 50ms delays.

**Typography System**: 
Pair Space Grotesk (headings, 700-900 weight) with Inter (body, 400-500 weight). Headings are large (48-72px desktop) with tight letter-spacing (-0.02em). Body text maintains generous line-height (1.7) for readability contrast.

**Design Movement**: Soft Modernism with Organic Flow

**Core Principles**:
- Curved, flowing shapes that mimic natural forms
- Layered transparency and frosted glass effects
- Gentle color transitions and ambient lighting
- Spacious layouts with breathing room between elements

**Color Philosophy**:
Build from a warm neutral base (cream #f5f3f0 and soft taupe #8b8680) with sage green (#6b8e7f) and warm terracotta (#d4896b) as primary accents. The palette evokes natural materials and sustainable living—perfect for smart home technology that integrates into daily life. Use subtle gradients (10-15% opacity shifts) to add depth.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Flowing, organic sections with curved dividers (SVG wave paths). Content follows natural reading patterns with generous whitespace. Hero section uses a soft diagonal composition (15-20 degrees) with rounded content cards that float over the background. Grid systems are flexible with varied card heights.

**Signature Elements**:
- Soft shadows with multiple layers (0 2px 8px rgba, 0 8px 24px rgba)
- Rounded corners everywhere (16-24px border-radius)
- Frosted glass cards with backdrop-filter: blur(12px) and subtle borders

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions feel gentle and responsive. Hover states lift elements with shadow expansion and subtle scale (1.02). Focus states glow with soft ring shadows. Micro-interactions provide feedback without aggression—think gentle pulses and smooth morphs.

**Animation**:
Smooth, organic timing with custom cubic-bezier curves (0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1). Elements fade and float in with combined opacity and translateY. Parallax scrolling on hero backgrounds (0.3-0.5 speed multiplier). Transition durations range 300-500ms for luxurious feel.

**Typography System**:
Outfit (headings, 500-700 weight) paired with Manrope (body, 400-600 weight). Both have rounded terminals that echo the design's soft geometry. Headings use comfortable sizes (40-56px desktop) with relaxed letter-spacing (0.01em). Generous line-height (1.8) throughout.

Deep space navy (#0a0e27) as the canvas with electric violet (#7c3aed) and cyan (#06b6d4) creating a futuristic glow. Use gradient overlays sparingly for depth (radial gradients from accent colors at 20% opacity). The palette suggests advanced technology and innovation. Borders and dividers use semi-transparent whites/cyans for circuit-board aesthetics.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Strict 12-column grid with visible grid lines as decorative elements. Hero section features a split-screen diagonal divide (45 degrees) with content on one side and animated grid visualization on the other. Sections are contained in bordered boxes that suggest technical blueprints. Asymmetric content placement within the grid structure.

**Signature Elements**:
- Glowing borders with box-shadow: 0 0 20px accent-color
- Grid overlay patterns (1px lines at 40px intervals, 10% opacity)
- Animated gradient borders using conic-gradient with rotation

Interactions feel precise and technical. Hover states trigger glow intensification and border color shifts. Buttons have animated gradient backgrounds that shift on hover. Click feedback includes brief flash effects. Elements feel like they're part of an interface system.

**Animation**:
Sharp, technical animations with ease-in-out timing. Grid elements reveal with staggered fade-ins (100ms intervals). Glow effects pulse subtly (2s duration, infinite). Page transitions use slide + fade combinations. Loading states show animated gradient progress bars.

**Typography System**:
JetBrains Mono (headings and accents, 600-700 weight) for technical feel, paired with DM Sans (body, 400-500 weight) for readability. Headings use monospace for futuristic aesthetic (36-48px desktop) with wide letter-spacing (0.05em). Code-style elements appear in UI labels and numbers.
